## 🏥 What is Medical-Research?

Medical-Research is a desktop application that helps medical professionals and researchers quickly find studies and clinical trials. It connects to trusted databases like PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, and Europe PMC. The app uses AI to search multiple sources at once. This saves time by reducing the need to visit each database separately.

Medical-Research helps you find relevant studies, clinical trials, and medical papers quickly. It shows summaries and important details in one place. This makes it easier to review literature without going through many websites.

## 🎯 How to Use Medical-Research

After launching the app, you can start searching medical literature:

1. Enter your search terms in the main search box. Use keywords related to your topic or patient condition.

2. Select which sources to search: PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, Europe PMC, or all.

3. Click the ‘Search’ button.

4. The app will gather matching studies and trials from each source.

5. Results show as a list with titles, authors, dates, and short summaries.

6. Click any result to see detailed information including:
   - Abstracts or study summaries
   - Trial status and dates
   - Links to full texts when available

7. Use filters on the results page to narrow your search by date, study type, or source.

8. You can save or export lists of studies for later review.
